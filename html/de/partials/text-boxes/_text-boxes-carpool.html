Wunder Carpool: Hamburg

A large european mobility company wants to launch a carpool service for their employees. All employees commute to the factory, which is located 30km away, in the outskirts of the city. Many employees commute alone in their cars.

Our multi-national team analysed where the employees commute from, to assure the needed liquidity and marketplace considerations to allow for relevant matching.

We offered a closed carpool system for the company for privacy and security reasons. The company is large enough to reach liquidity. We suggest to launch a private beta pilot as a proof of concept. Wunder offered dedicated launch support including hyperlocal marketing and growth support.
