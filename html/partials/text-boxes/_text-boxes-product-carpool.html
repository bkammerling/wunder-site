Carpooling as a solution.

Wunder empowers communities to commute safely and convenienty.

ID Verification

Each user can verify their IDs in the app. This keeps users in control of who they carpool with and ensures the carpooling community remains secure.

Live Tracking

Passengers can track their driver's locations and all the trips, past, present and future can be seen in a comprehensive backend admin system.

Wunder Wallet

A reliable transaction system gives end-users a more convenient payment method. New digital payment systems can be added depending on the country.

Researched Design

The interface has been developed over 2 years of continuous research. Down to the colors and fonts used, we ensure the best experience for our carpoolers.
