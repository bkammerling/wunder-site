WUNDER AUTOTECH MEETUP

We are hosting a quarterly informal, invitation-only meet-up for leaders of the European AutoTech scene. Our mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable transportation. By providing a platform to startups, corporates, NGOs and research institutions to share and build on each others ideas we hope to get there faster.

Date & Place

A quarterly, informal, week-day evening gathering hosted at the Wunder HQ in Hamburg, Germany.

Speeches

3 Speakers each give a 15-minute "off the record" talk, sharing their bold idea about the future of mobility.

Networking

Talks are followed by informal networking with food, drinks & entertainment.
