Executive Bios

Gunnar Froh

CEO & Founder

Gunnar Froh is the founder and CEO of Wunder Carpool (formerly WunderCar), a technology company from Hamburg and part of the new German automotive industry. Before Wunder was founded in 2014, Gunnar was part of the leadership team at Airbnb, the forerunner of the sharing economy from San Francisco, after they acquired his own start-up in 2011. Previously, Gunnar was a consultant at McKinsey & Company. He graduated with a diploma in business from WHU - Otto Beisheim School of Management (M.Sc.), studied at HEC Montréal and SMU Dallas and participated as a Teaching Assistant at WHU in Koblenz and Kellogg School of Management in Chicago in their PhD programs.

Samuel Baker

COO

Samuel Baker is the COO (since 2014) of Wunder Carpool (formerly WunderCar), a technology company from Hamburg and part of the new German automotive industry. After working at eBay and Adidas he founded the Latin American startup Computod@s before he joined as a partner at Just Business, a seed stage incubator and VC in San Francisco. Originally from America, Samuel has extensive experience as an entrepreneur in both the non-profit and for-profit sectors and as an investor in disruptive technologies and consumer brands. Samuel holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Commerce Finance, Music and International Business from Santa Clara University.

David Jeusette

CTO

David Jeusette is the CTO (since 2016) of Wunder Carpool (formerly WunderCar), a technology company from Hamburg and part of the new German automotive industry. He has more than eight years experience in the mobility industry and founded the platforms Djengo and Djump, among others, working as the CTO for the management of the technical teams. David has many years experience as a developer and completed his studies in Software Engineering (M.Sc.) at the Université catholique de Louvain with magna cum laude.
