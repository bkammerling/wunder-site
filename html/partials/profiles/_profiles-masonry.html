Past speakers

Paulin Dementhon

Founder & CEO Drivy

Paulin Dementhon is CEO and Founder of Drivy. Drivy is Europe's leading platform to rent cars between individuals. He started creating a carpooling service in 2009 without any knowledge of the web before launching Drivy in 2010.

Drivy operates today in 5 countries with a total of 42,000 cars to rent and more than 1.2 million users.

Jonas Gumny

COO mytaxi

Jonas joined mytaxi in April 2013. He is the COO of mytaxi since November 2015 and holds a degree in industrial engineering from KIT, while also studying at the University of Queensland and the ETH Zurich.

Based in Germany, Jonas is responsible for leading the global mytaxi operations, is driving expansion across Europe and responsible for global Sales activities.

Dr.-Ing. Florian Herrmann

Head of Competence Center “Mobility Innovation” at Fraunhofer IAO

Florian Herrmann heads the Fraunhofer “Mobility Innovation” center since 2015 and runs the Ambient Mobility Lab, a joint initiative with the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) and Fraunhofer institute. Florian studied business engineering at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology and received his Ph.D. in technology management. He pioneers mobility research to analyze, design and develop sustainable mobility systems that interact with future urban systems.

Ehud Levy

General Partner at Canaan Partners Israel

In over two decades in the hi-tech industry as an entrepreneur, investor and operator, Ehud has established a leadership position in the Israeli market, especially in the mobile sector.

Prior to joining Canaan Partners Israel, Ehud spent seven years as a Managing Partner at Vertex Venture Capital. Among other successful investments, Ehud identified Waze as a bright star from the start, leading the first investment round in the company and serving on its board of directors until its acquisition by Google for over $1 Billion in 2013. Ehud is heading the fund’s investments in digital media technology & applications.

Michel Stumpe

Founder & CEO at Free2Move

Michel joined the mobility revolution by founding Carjump, a carsharing app which allowed users to reserve and book cars across several car sharing providers.

Carjump was acquired by the PSA Group and was rebranded to Free2Move in 2017. At Free2Move, Michel drives the digitalization of mobility services, by redefining the way people share, access and utilise vehicles.

Urs Rahne

Partner at BCG Digital Ventures

Urs Rahne joined BCG Digital Ventures, BCG’s venture capital and incubation firm in early 2014, right at inception. He focuses on tech plays in the mobility space. Most recently, Urs acted as General Manager for COUP, the Bosch Group’s shared mobility platform.
